平台现有机制

* 平台没有审查和禁止特定信息，但已调用AI解析语义，将价值观不符的信息往下沉、少展示。这是概率问题，如果总体输入里优质信息不多，您仍会看得到有误导倾向的信息，请保留戒心。

* 平台已禁用图片展示，这样可从源头上大幅减少诱惑。已在算法上鼓励以兴趣爱好为纽带建立点对点连接，但Dot2Dot只是起点，走向哪里靠个人，靠缘分。别急着得到什么，我们就能大幅免疫。

Dot2Dot Algorithm:

* No censorship or blocking of specific content. However, AI analyzes semantics and demotes value-inconsistent posts — they appear less often. This is probabilistic. If the overall input lacks quality information, you may still see misleading content. Stay vigilant.

* Images are disabled — drastically reducing temptations at the source. The algorithm encourages interest-based, peer-to-peer connections. But that's just the starting point. Where it goes depends on you, and on fate. Don't rush for outcomes — that's how we stay largely immune.

平台现有机制

* 用户每天可以发出N条广播，但平台每天对每个用户只新增一条展示，其余放在队列里依次展示在平台。Dot2Dot是慢车马，是这氛围，也能省资源。

* 用户的第21条广播被展示出来时，他的第1条会被删除，也就是FIFO，他的展示上限是20条。久远事，不留痕。

Dot2Dot Algorithm:

* Each user can send unlimited broadcasts daily, but the platform only surfaces one per user per day. The rest go into a queue and are shown over time. Dot2Dot moves at a slow pace — it's the vibe, and it saves resources.

* When a user's 21st broadcast is shown, their 1st is deleted. FIFO, 20 posts max — old things leave no trace.
